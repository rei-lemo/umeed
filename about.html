About

UMEED means hope.

A B Samman Initiative is designed to bring dignity, recognition, and trusted opportunity to blue-collar workers through verified digital identity.

For workers

Proof of identity, skill, and experience in one portable profile.

For employers

A reliable way to discover people whose skills have been reviewed.

For government

Better visibility into local skills, employment demand, and programme outcomes.
