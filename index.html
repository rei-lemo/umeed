How It Works

From registration to recognition

Three steps stand between any worker and a verified, government-recognised identity.

01

Register & Upload Documents

Workers sign up and submit identity and trade documents for review.

02

Skill Verification

A practical assessment confirms the worker's trade and skill level.

03

Digital Worker ID

Verified workers receive a portable ID and appear to employers searching their trade.
